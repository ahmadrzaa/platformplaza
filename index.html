Introducing the latest Co-working Space at Platform Plaza : A Space for Innovation and Collaboration. Located adjacent to BuQua Near Manama City, Bahrain, the bustling heart of innovation and creativity, Platform Plaza Workhub is a state-of-the-art co-working space set to redefine the way professionals work, collaborate, and thrive.

Conveniently accessible by public transportation, including the Expo Metro Line, it offers unparalleled connectivity for individuals andbusinesses seeking a strategic location. But it’s not just about the space; it’s about the people. At our co-working hub, we foster a community- centric atmosphere where like-minded individuals can connect, collaborate, and grow together.
